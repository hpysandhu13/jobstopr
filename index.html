Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you a licensed immigration consultant?
No. We are not licensed RCICs or immigration lawyers. We share publicly available information from IRCC and provincial government websites, and we help with job search, resume tailoring, and interview prep. For legal advice on your specific case, you must consult a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC) registered with CICC, or a Canadian immigration lawyer.
Can my warehouse job lead to PR?
It depends on the NOC code and TEER level. Most general warehouse jobs are TEER 5 and do not qualify directly for Express Entry CEC. However, you can move into TEER 3 or 2 supervisor roles, or use specific OINP streams. Our free guide walks through this in detail.
Do you guarantee a job or PR?
No. Anyone who guarantees you a job or PR is lying or scamming you. We provide tools, templates, and strategy. The work is yours. Outcomes depend on your effort, qualifications, and current immigration policies — none of which we control.
Is your support really in Punjabi and Hindi?
Yes. Our social content (Instagram, TikTok) is primarily Punjabi and Hindi voiceover with English text. 1-on-1 calls can be in Punjabi, Hindi, or English. PDF guides are written in clear English so you can save them or share them with family.
What if I'm not in the GTA?
Our content is GTA-focused but most of the principles (NOC codes, federal Express Entry, language testing) apply anywhere in Canada. Provincial program guidance specifically covers Ontario. If you're in BC, Alberta, or other provinces, the federal pathways still apply.
Can I get a refund?
Digital products (Mini Kit, Starter Kit) are non-refundable once downloaded due to the nature of digital goods. Premium Kit refunds are available before the personalized resume work begins. 1-on-1 calls are refundable if cancelled 24+ hours in advance.
